CalDenS Help

Introduction

CalDenS is an app developed by Juan Eduardo Riva having the purpose of facilitating the calcs commonly used in planting crops.

The app was developed thinking that the user is working in the field where commonly the communications have poor quality and that's why it does not relay on internet or other data connection to do his work. Also the app allow the modification of the variables on the fly, facilitating have a complete view of the alternatives in the variables of the selected crop.

Using CalDenS.

Booting CalDenS.

In the menu of your blackberry you will see the icon of CalDenS. The icon represents a ruler and the seed in the floor. Please click the icon to begin using the app.

Soon you will be confronted with the splash screen of the app showing main email. By the way, if you want to announce your product or company use that channel of communication to reach me. After four seconds the splash page will re direct you to the language selection page.

Selecting Your language.

The language selection page gives you the chance to select the measure system that feet you better too. English users have two options, those are choosing between "English Metric" and "English Royal".

Selecting Your Crop.

Now you are un steep near the visualization of your result. Please choose the crop that you are planting.
